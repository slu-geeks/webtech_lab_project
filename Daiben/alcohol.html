Encounters with alcoholics

I had interviewed some people regarding "Why do the love to drink?" and these are common answers:

  • It serves as a good time when you're stressed.
  • It serves as a cure to your problems.
  • It makes you look cool.
  • Because all of your friends are drinking.

IT SERVES A GOOD TIME WHEN YOU'RE STRESSED.

We all know that being addicted to alcohol is a bad habit, and being addicted to it is considered a drug. But why do people get so addicted with alcohols? It is because sometimes especially men find this way as a good time because of being stressed. They thought that by drinking it will lessen their stress. But the truth is setting alcohol as good time will lead them to get drunk and do crazy things which they won't remember it after all.

IT SERVES A CURE TO YOUR PROBLEMS.

Also they considered alcohol as the cure to their problems because when they drink alcohol they already think that they can forget all their little and big problems, but the truth is even you drink a lot of alcohol just to cure all the problems you are encountering, you will never escape it. It is because is just the side effect of the alcohol and if you woke up the next day the problem will still be there.

IT MAKES YOU LOOK COOL

Some people also considered alcohol as being cool, most especially this generation especially the youth thinks that all the bad habits are cool, but they're wrong, because the truth is alcohol won't make you cool at all, in fact it will make you uncool especially when you get drunk like being loud, telling something that aren't true.

BECAUSE ALL OF YOUR FRIENDS ARE DRINKING.

Another one is they love alcohol because of all of his/her friends are drinking. I think it is not an excuse to drink just because your friend drinks, because if your friends are real friends if they know that you don't drink. They won't let you drink, and it doesn't matter if you're the only one who doesn't drink because you can be healthier than them.


In order to prevent the addiction over alcohol drinks, there are many things that should regard;

  • Tell to your love ones and friends that you are now trying to prevent drinking alcoholic drinks.
  • Set your goals or list of what to do to avoid thinking of you want to drink.
  • Avoid Temptation

TELL TO YOUR LOVE ONES AND FRIEND THAT YOU ARE NOW TRYING TO PREVENT DRINKING ALCOHOLIC DRINKS.

When you started to think of quitting, the first step you need to do is tell it to all the people that surrounds you most especially your family, friends, officemates or even your neighbors. The essence of telling or sharing this to them is it will really let them know about what are your plans. In this case, it will help you because they know what are the things that should be avoided. It will really help you especially in escaping your addiction of drinking.
