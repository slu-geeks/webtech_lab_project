Why do people loves to drink alcohol?

We have interviewed some people regarding "Why do the love to drink?" and these are common answers:

  • It serves as a good time when you're stressed.
  • It serves as a cure to your problems.
  • It makes you look cool.
  • Because all of your friends are drinking.

IT SERVES A GOOD TIME WHEN YOU'RE STRESSED.

We all know that being addicted to alcohol is a bad habit, and being addicted to it is considered drug abuse. But why do people get so addicted with alcohols? It is because sometimes, especially among men, find drinking a good time because of stress. They think that by drinking, it will lessen their stress. But the truth is: using alcohol as a good time will lead them to get drunk and do crazy things which they won't the next day.

IT SERVES AS A CURE TO YOUR PROBLEMS.

Some people have considered alcohol as the cure to their problems because when they drink alcohol, they think that they would forget all their little and big problems, but the truth is even you drink a lot of alcohol just to forget about all the problems you are encountering, you will never escape it. This is just another side effect of alcohol ; if you woke up the next day the problem will still be there.

IT MAKES YOU LOOK COOL

Some people consider drinking alcohol as cool, most especially this generation; the youth thinks that all the bad habits are cool, but they're wrong, because the truth is alcohol won't make you cool at all. In fact it will make you uncool especially when you get drunk and do things like get loud or telling something to someone that isn't true.

BECAUSE ALL OF YOUR FRIENDS ARE DRINKING.

Another reason is that people love alcohol because of all of their drinking friends. I think it is not an excuse to drink just because your friend drinks, because if your friends are real friends and they know that you don't drink. , they won't let you drink, and it doesn't matter if you're the only one who doesn't drink because you'll be healthier than them.
