How You can help us ?

Of course, you can help us in our quest for substance abuse awareness! If you've been a victim of substance abuse at the past and now want to help people who are suffering from that with your experiences, you can always join to our network for doing voluntarily jobs and help.

You can also trust us and introduce those who are your friends and you're love ones who are suffering from addiction or any kind of abuse. We'll keep these data secret and we can try to help him/her to quit and recover himself/herself.

If you want to support these people you can also contact us and donate in out account and we'll spend your money for helping these people and your help might save a person from death.
