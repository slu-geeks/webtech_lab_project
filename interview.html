Interview with a smoker

Why use cigarettes?

- One friend told me that he uses cigarettes, because he saw it from his other friends, he thinks that it's cool until he realizes that he's being addicted to it and keep on smoking with his friends.

-Another friend said that it is a bond between friends if you smoke cigarettes, you all come along and smoke cigarettes when it's a break time and you share your ideas or converse with them and it is fun so they smoke and get addicted or get used to it.

-Another told me that it can take all your problems away, like it can temporarily cure stress and everything, it can also help solve problems in your situation. It can be addicting because it's a temporary escape from stress and their problems that is why they are addicted to it and until now, they smoke cigarettes without knowing the effects on their body. They only focus on the mind and not the body.
